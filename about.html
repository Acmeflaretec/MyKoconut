About Us - MyKoconut

Welcome to MyKoconut, your trusted source for 100% pure coconut oil. Our journey began with a vision to create a business that not only provides a good source of income but also contributes to the well-being of our customers. After the unfortunate failure of two previous business ventures, we were determined to find a sustainable and meaningful path forward.

During our travels, we discovered the alarming production of unrefined coconut oil, which poses serious health risks, including deadly diseases like cancer. Witnessing this sparked a realization: there was a pressing need for pure, unadulterated coconut oil in the market. This led to the birth of MyKoconut, a venture dedicated to offering the highest quality coconut oil to our valued customers.

At MyKoconut, we source our coconuts from the best farms in Kerala, renowned for their superior quality. Our oil is extracted from these premium coconuts and is strictly unadulterated, ensuring that you receive only the purest product. We take pride in our meticulous process, which involves careful selection, extraction, and distribution from our own mill.

Our mission is to provide a healthier alternative to harmful, unrefined oils. We believe in the natural goodness of coconut oil and its numerous benefits for health and wellness. With MyKoconut, you can trust that every drop is pure, safe, and of the highest quality.
